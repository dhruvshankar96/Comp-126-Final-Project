There is a place for everyone at the University of North Carolina's Navy Reserve Officer Corps unit. We encourage everyone and anyone to apply, reguardless of prior experience!


High school students should apply to the University and to the US Navy four year scholarship. This can be found on Navy.mil. Create a private portal and select either Navy or Marine Corps! Once you are accepted to both the school and scholarship, it is a simple process to link the two. We hope to see you soon!


College students should come by to the unit and speak to one of the active duty advsiors. College students are allowed to particpate in ROTC activities, recieve training and guidance, and boost their resume through training despite not having a scholarship. The unit will help each and every college student prepare a package and will do their utmost to turn all college programmer students into contracted students by their junior year.


Prior enlisted personnel should put in a package at their current commands. The MECEP and OC programs are often overlooked in the trianing pipeline. If any enlisted personnel has any question on the pipeline, please contact us through the email link so we cna put you in touch with the active duty Marine on station.
