Careers in the Naval Service

Midshipmen have the option of commisioning into four different warfare communities: Surface, Submarine, Aviation, and Marine Corps. Each community is represented by one of our active duty staff members, to give the students a better perspective on which career they wish to choose. Our midshipman are divided into two groups while they are here. Navy Options train to the US Navy standards and have the option of selecting Surface, Submarine, or Aviation. These students are ranked against each other and their peers across the country based on military aptitude, physical fitness, and academics. Their standing decides their order for selection. Our other students fall under the title Marine Option. Marine Options are not ranked in the same way as their peers.These students train and abide by Marine Corps standards, which includes apperance conduct and physical fitness requirements. These students are not ranked until they complete schooling and attend The Basic School, where they are evaluated based on their infantry prowess. Their class ranking decides which Marine Occupational Specialty (MOS) they will be able to select upon graduation from The Basic School. No matter the job, our midshipmen are prepared and ready to fight the wars of the future and lead their sailors and Marines into harms way and back again.
